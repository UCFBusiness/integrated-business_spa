Integrated Business Program

Get to The ONE!

Beginning in 2015, UCF-CBA raised the standards for all of the degree programs in the college, while providing an educational experience that better reflects the demands of the marketplace. Our objective is to help our students "get to the one" - become the person that gets the job...starts a new business...makes a contribution...gives back...wins the contract.

In the end, business is all about "getting to the one" and our curriculum, programs, and events are all designed to equip our students to achieve the objective.

Today, students must prepare for a more diversified economy that offers shorter- term employment prospects and values customization, connectivity and speed. Companies need people who can innovate by connecting the dots, communicate effectively with diverse groups of people and assume different roles as the firm responds to a turbulent marketplace.




This demand require employees who understand the entire enterprise, see opportunities in emerging trends, and can pull together diverse resources to create value. As such, business school faculty must work in new ways to provide the kind of education that produces such "integrators" by changing the culture, structure, and substance of the business school experience.

The UCF College of Business Administration is creating the right environment, employing the best faculty, and facilitating innovative programs to give students a competitive advantage in the world of business now and in the future. The Integrated Business Major has been developed in partnership with local business leaders and alumni who are searching for people that demonstrate the following skills and traits.

  • Strong communicators and collaborators who can work with and lead teams composed of people with diverse backgrounds and experiences.
  • Able to collect, analyze and act on real time data in ways that create competitive advantage.
  • Comfortable working outside of their comfort zone and are willing to take smart risks.
  • Effective and innovative problem solvers who know how to take lessons learned in one environment and successfully apply them in a different context.

These qualities transcend "majors" and will be a competitive edge for our students as they compete for their jobs, build varied careers and aspire to leadership.

The University of Central Florida College of Business Administration (UCF CBA) is pioneering the new business school experience. Faculty, alums, prospective employers and friends of the college all contributed to a new program and structure that will better equip graduates to lead in the marketplace - today and in the future. In addition, our programs have all raised the bar on expectations, accountability and performance of our faculty and students, creating a more competitive and challenging educational experience.

The UCF CBA delivers on its vision and mission through its four unique strengths and qualities. Experience, culture, leadership and place define and determine the success of our students, stakeholders, and community.

All of these are closely linked to the UCF CBA. In addition, UCF is the second largest university in the United States, providing an unparalleled opportunity to work with the most diverse and dynamic student body.

Student Experience

We are providing students with the experiences in and out of the classroom that establish a culture to instill the four attributes (communicator/collaborator; risk taker; decision-maker; problem solver) that will ensure their professional success.

Entrepreneurial Culture

UCF CBA has been a thought leader and part of the foundation for the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Orlando. From the Joust - business plan competition; to the UCF Blackstone Launchpad - ideation and business modeling center; students have access to services and opportunities to acquire and perfect the skills of the entrepreneur

Thought Leadership

The UCF CBA, faculty and staff represent the thought leadership in business across all disciplines and majors. In addition, UCF CBA Alumni and Hall of Fame members are leading businesses throughout the world and come back to share their experiences and knowledge with students and the community.

Unique Place of Opportunity

Orlando is one of the world's fastest growing economic centers and a place that is widely recognized as one of the top travel destinations. This community has some of the most interesting and exciting new economic centers from Lake Nona's Medical City to the Creative and Health Villages in Downtown Orlando. While is travel and tourism economy is among the most well-known, there are significant technology, simulation, gaming, healthcare and IT sectors.
